Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 31, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Chaptiva (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles information while providing multi‑platform video content pack generation (transcript, chapters, hooks, titles, SEO copy, clip ideas, captions). This beta is evolving; we update practices as features ship. Material changes are signaled via email or an on‑site banner.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email Address: Submitted via forms to deliver packs and send critical product updates (you can opt out of non‑essential messages).
  • Video URL: Public or unlisted identifiers for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram / Reels, or Shorts. We do not store the actual video content—only references and derived text assets.
  • Generated Assets: Transcript, chapters, SEO description, keyword phrases, title variants, hooks, clip ideas, captions, internal JSON pack structure.
  • Operational & Diagnostic Logs: Timestamps, processing duration, internal errors, resource usage (no unnecessary personal info).
  • Optional Form Metadata: Content type (long/short) or second language preference.

2. How We Use Data

  • Generate and deliver your requested content pack.
  • Improve segmentation / generation quality (aggregate & anonymized trends only).
  • Communicate material service updates or issues.
  • Detect and prevent abuse (rate limiting, anomaly detection).

3. What We Do Not Do

  • No resale or licensing of user transcripts or generated outputs.
  • No training of third‑party foundation models on your raw transcripts.
  • No ad network tracking pixels (we may add privacy‑centric analytics like Plausible).

4. AI / Model Usage

We use local / open‑source speech-to-text (Whisper variants) and lightweight generation models when possible. Heavier third‑party LLM calls (if any) are limited to prompt scopes referencing only the content necessary for the requested asset. Sensitive segments may be truncated or filtered before being sent to external inference APIs (if used).

5. Legal Basis (If Applicable to Your Jurisdiction)

  • Consent: You submit a URL and email voluntarily.
  • Legitimate Interest: Operating, securing, and improving a repurposing workflow tool.
  • Performance of a Contract: Delivering the pack you requested.

6. Retention & Deletion

Generated assets are retained to allow re‑delivery and quality iteration. You may request deletion by emailing contact@chaptiva.app. Backups (if implemented) are short‑lived and rotate. Deletion requests remove active copies and associated pack JSON within a reasonable period unless retention is required for legal or security reasons.

7. Security

  • Transport security (HTTPS).
  • Access minimization (only necessary internal operators).
  • Separation of environment credentials (not stored in repo).
  • Progressive hardening—beta means potential architecture shifts; we will tighten controls as we scale.

8. Third‑Party Services

  • Form Handling: Formspree / similar (captures submitted form fields).
  • Email Delivery: SendGrid / Brevo (later in automation phase) to send “pack ready” notifications.
  • Payments: Stripe (stores billing info; we do not see full card data).
  • Analytics (optional): Plausible or comparable privacy‑centric platform (aggregated, no personal profiles).

9. International Transfers

Infrastructure and third‑party services may process data in multiple regions. We rely on commonly used cloud providers and their standard contractual protections where applicable.

10. Children

Not directed to individuals under 16. If you believe we inadvertently processed such data, contact us; we will remove it.

11. Your Choices

  • Opt Out of Non‑Essential Emails: Use unsubscribe links or contact us.
  • Deletion: Email deletion request to contact@chaptiva.app.
  • Data Access: You may request a summary of stored generated assets linked to your email.

12. Changes

We will revise this document as features evolve. Material changes: notice banner or direct email to active users.

13. Contact

Email: contact@chaptiva.app

This policy is not a substitute for legal counsel; adapt for your jurisdiction as you scale.
