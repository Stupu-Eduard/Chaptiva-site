How It Works

Unified flow for any link—slightly different branches for long vs short content.

1. Submit

Drop URL + email + content type (long / short).

2. Transcribe

Audio extracted & transcribed; short video path skips chapter logic.

3. Generate

Titles, hooks, clip ideas, SEO copy & optional translation.

4. Deliver

Pack emailed (JSON + text files + SRT). Roadmap: instant download page & history.

FAQ

Common early questions across niches (fitness, coding, language, lifestyle, coaching, finance etc.).

Does it only work for tutorials?

No—any clear spoken content (fitness tips, lifestyle advice, business breakdowns, etc.). Chapters auto only if video is long enough.

Works for TikTok & Reels?

Yes—short videos skip the chapter step and generate booster assets (hooks, variants, repurpose suggestions).

Private / unlisted videos?

Unlisted accessible URLs work. Private videos requiring auth are not supported in MVP.

Why one free video?

Enough to prove time saved & output quality before you decide to continue.

Accuracy & clean-up?

We do a manual pass during early beta to polish obvious errors & formatting.

Languages for captions?

English base + Spanish first. More languages prioritized by user votes.

Need channel logins?

No logins or API keys required for MVP—just the video link.
